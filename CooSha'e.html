why choose us?

Choosing Coosha'e Cares is both a practical decision but also a cost-effective one. Home health care is generally less expensive than hospital stays or skilled nursing facilities. The convenience of receiving care at home means that patients can maintain their routines and stay close to their loved ones, which can significantly enhance their overall well-being and recovery process.

With Coosha'e Cares, you can rest assured that your health and comfort are our top priorities.

Founded by Expert Black Healthcare Professionals

Shantae Coppock, a Philadelphia native and Doctor of Philosophy, founded this company with the goal of providing compassionate, high-quality care for aging loved ones and family members. With a background in managing law enforcement and public safety professionals, as well as teaching, Dr. Coppock applied her organizational strategy and problem-solving skills to address the individual needs of elderly care. She focused on creating a company that can both help and meet the challenges of aging with compassionate service.

Now partnered with Amina T. Harris, a seasoned nurse leader with an MBA, MSN, and RN credentials, they combine their expertise in healthcare and business administration to run this company as proud Black healthcare professionals. With their shared passion and leadership, their office staff—who have years of experience in various care areas—are dedicated to helping families navigate the care process. Whether answering questions or scheduling consultations, the staff is committed to ensuring that seniors can safely and practically remain in the comfort of their own homes.

Our services

Available Services & Specialized Programs

We offer a range of personalized home care services to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Personal Care
Assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and other personal hygiene tasks.
Companionship
Engaging in conversation, playing games, reading, and other activities to provide emotional support and alleviate loneliness.
Meal Preparation
Planning and preparing nutritious meals that meet our clients' dietary needs and preferences.
Medication Management
Ensuring that our clients take their medications on time and in the correct dosage.
Light Housekeeping
Keeping our clients' homes clean and tidy, including dusting, vacuuming, and laundry.
Transportation
Providing transportation to medical appointments, social events, and other outings.
